About me

Hello, welcome to my website.

I am Lili Tong, a Ph.D student from INSA Lyon, laboratory LIRIS. My primary research interest is in the area of Human-Computer Interaction, which is focusing on exploring interaction, collaboration, and applications in multi-surface environments (imagine an environment that combines different devices, such as tabletops, wall displays, tablets, mobile phones, and also PCs).

As a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, I have the background of Electrical Engineering (M.S.) and Physics (B.S.). Studies in different domains made me have a quite open mind. I'm interested in different kinds of new technologies, especially the ones that can facilitate our daily lives.
