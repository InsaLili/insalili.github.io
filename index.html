About me

Hello, welcome to my website.

I am Lili Tong, a Human-Computer Interaction Researcher at Lenovo Research. I am working with a group of talented researchers, designers and developers to explore potentials, seek solutions, and improve user experience in Augmented Reality.

Before my work in Lenovo, I got my Ph.D. from INSA Lyon, CNRS, laboratory LIRIS. My primary research interest is in the area of Human-Computer Interaction, which focuses on exploring interaction, collaboration, and applications in multi-surface environments (imagine an environment that combines different devices, such as tabletops, wall displays, tablets, mobile phones, and also PCs).

Besides my Ph.D. in Computer Science, I also have a master's degree in Electrical Engineering and bachelor's degree in Physics. Studies in different domains made me have a quite open mind. I'm interested in different kinds of new technologies, especially the ones that can facilitate our daily lives.
